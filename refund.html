Refund Policy
Last updated: July 1, 2026 · Applies to all NexusOS subscriptions

✓ We offer a 14-day free trial on all plans — no credit card required. Try NexusOS fully before you pay.

1. Our Commitment

We want you to be completely satisfied with NexusOS. Because we offer a full-featured 14-day free trial before any payment is required, we encourage you to thoroughly evaluate the platform before subscribing. This policy explains your refund rights for paid subscriptions.

2. Refund Eligibility

You may request a full refund within 7 days of your first paid charge if:

  • It is your first subscription payment (not a renewal)
  • You have not used the paid features extensively (reasonable use during evaluation is acceptable)
  • You contact us within the 7-day window at jhun07.velado@gmail.com

3. Renewal Charges

Monthly subscription renewals are non-refundable. If you wish to stop being charged, you must cancel your subscription before your next renewal date. You can cancel at any time from your NexusOS account settings under Pricing & Plans. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain access until that date.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within the eligible window:

  • Email jhun07.velado@gmail.com with the subject line: "Refund Request — NexusOS"
  • Include the email address associated with your account and the reason for your request
  • We will respond within 2 business days and process approved refunds within 5–10 business days

For international payments processed by Paddle, refunds are issued back to the original payment method. For Philippine payments processed by PayMongo, refunds follow PayMongo's processing timeline.

5. Exceptions — Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Renewal charges (monthly subscription renewals after the first payment)
  • Requests made after 7 days of the initial charge
  • Accounts suspended or terminated for violating our Terms of Service
  • Partial months — we do not issue prorated refunds for unused days within a billing cycle
  • Failure to cancel before a renewal date

6. Disputes and Chargebacks

If you have a billing concern, please contact us first at jhun07.velado@gmail.com before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer. Most issues can be resolved quickly. Fraudulent chargebacks may result in account suspension.

For payments processed by Paddle, you may also contact Paddle's support directly at paddle.com as they are the Merchant of Record for international transactions.

7. Plan Downgrades

If you downgrade from a higher plan to a lower plan mid-cycle, the downgrade takes effect at the start of the next billing period. No prorated refund is issued for the remaining days on the higher plan.

8. Service Disruptions

In the event of significant, extended service outages (exceeding 72 consecutive hours) caused by factors within our control, we will offer affected subscribers a pro-rated credit toward a future billing cycle. We will not offer cash refunds for minor disruptions or outages caused by third-party providers.

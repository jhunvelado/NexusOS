Common questions

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?
No. Your 14-day free trial starts the moment you create an account. No credit card required until you choose to upgrade.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. No penalties, no questions.
What payment methods do you accept?
International payments are processed by Paddle and accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal. Philippine customers can also pay via GCash, Maya, and QR Ph through PayMongo.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a 7-day refund on your first subscription charge if you're not satisfied. Renewals are non-refundable. See our full Refund Policy for details.
Is my data secure?
Yes. NexusOS uses Supabase with Row-Level Security, ensuring your workspace data is completely isolated. All data is encrypted in transit. 2FA is available on all accounts.
Who is NexusOS for?
NexusOS is built for the CEO + EA team unit — where the executive and their assistant operate from the same platform. It's ideal for SMB founders, C-suite leaders, private equity executives, and healthcare executives who need one system to manage all their operations.

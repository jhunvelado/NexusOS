FAQ

Do I need a credit card to trial?
No. 14-day trial starts immediately, no card required. You only enter payment details when you subscribe.
What happens when trial ends?
You'll be prompted to choose a plan. If you don't subscribe, account goes read-only and data is retained 30 days.
What currency are prices in?
USD. International payments via Paddle (handles local currency + VAT/GST automatically). Philippines in PHP via PayMongo.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes — 7-day refund on your first payment. Renewals non-refundable. See our Refund Policy.
Is Google Calendar integration free?
Google Calendar sync is available on Professional and Enterprise plans. It uses OAuth — no passwords shared, read-only access, revoke anytime.
Is NexusOS GDPR compliant?
Yes. iubenda cookie consent, Supabase secure storage, and Paddle (EU-certified) for payments. See our Privacy Policy.

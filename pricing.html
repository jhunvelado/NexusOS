Frequently asked questions

Do I need a credit card to start the trial?
No. Your 14-day trial starts immediately with no credit card required. You only enter payment details when you choose to subscribe.
What happens when my trial ends?
After 14 days, you'll be prompted to choose a plan. If you don't subscribe, your account switches to read-only mode and your data is retained for 30 days before deletion.
Can I change plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from Settings → Subscription. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the next renewal date.
What currency are prices in?
All prices are in USD. International customers pay via Paddle which handles local currency conversion and taxes (EU VAT, GST, etc.) automatically. Philippine customers can pay in PHP via PayMongo.
What payment methods do you accept?
International: Visa, Mastercard, PayPal (via Paddle). Philippines: GCash, Maya, QR Ph, credit/debit cards (via PayMongo).
Is there a refund policy?
Yes — we offer a 7-day refund on your first payment if you're not satisfied. Renewal charges are non-refundable, but you can cancel anytime before renewal. See our Refund Policy for full details.
Is NexusOS GDPR compliant?
Yes. We use iubenda for cookie consent and privacy compliance, Supabase for secure data storage, and Paddle (EU-certified) for international payments. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

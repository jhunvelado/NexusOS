New in v5.0

Executive Focus Card

Every time you open the dashboard, NexusOS surfaces the single most important item requiring your attention — overdue commitments, pending decisions, critical tasks. One thing. Right now.

New in v5.0

Commitment Tracker

Every promise made in a meeting, logged and tracked to completion. Risk badges flag broken promises before they damage relationships — At Risk, Relationship Risk, and Execution Risk indicators built in.

New in v5.0

Claude AI Briefing

Powered by Claude (Anthropic). Every morning, real AI analyses your live workspace — tasks, commitments, approvals, relationships — and delivers a concise executive briefing with your top 3 actions for the day.

New in v5.0

Travel Risk Indicators

Live weather, wind, and conditions for every upcoming trip destination. Colour-coded risk alerts — green, yellow, red — so you know what you're flying into before you leave the office.

Approval Centre

Every request from your EA — travel, expenses, legal, events — comes here with full context. Amount, reason, category. Approve or reject with one tap. No more digging through email threads.

Travel Hub

Leg-by-leg itinerary, hotel confirmations, key contacts, AI pre-trip brief, and expense tracking against your approved budget. Everything in one place before you board.

PE Dashboard

Built for PE-backed operators. Track every investor relationship, deal status, board member follow-up cadence, and LOI timeline. Nothing drifts quietly.

Executive Chat

Private CEO-EA workspace. Real-time messaging, all history saved. Replaces fragmented WhatsApp threads. Every message tied to context — no noise, no missing updates.

Relationship Intel

Key investors, board members, and strategic partners tracked with follow-up cadence, relationship warmth, private notes, and overdue flags. Managed by your EA, visible to you.

Shared Task Management

One task list. Both CEO and EA see the same live view. Priority tags, due dates, status, owner. Nothing falls through. Everything has an owner and a due date.

Meeting Prep AI

Auto-generated briefings for every meeting: key participants, objectives, required documents, and a prep checklist. You arrive prepared without doing any research yourself.

Intelligence Centre

Live risk monitor. Real-time view of overdue tasks, pending decisions, missed follow-ups, and meeting readiness. Warning banners for anything critical. You're never caught off guard.

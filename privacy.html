3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, operate, and improve the NexusOS service
  • To authenticate you and secure your account
  • To process subscription payments via our payment processors
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, billing notices)
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents

We do not use your data to train AI models. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. AI Features

NexusOS includes AI-powered features (such as executive briefings and approval recommendations) powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you use these features, relevant context from your workspace (such as task summaries or approval titles) may be sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. This data is processed transiently and is not used by Anthropic to train their models under their standard API terms. We recommend not entering sensitive personal or confidential information into AI-powered fields.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider with Row-Level Security (RLS) enforced at the database level. This means your workspace data is isolated and inaccessible to other users.

Security measures we maintain include:

  • Encrypted data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Row-Level Security ensuring users can only access their own workspace data
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) available for all accounts
  • Session timeouts after 15 minutes of inactivity
  • Audit logging of key actions within your workspace

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate NexusOS:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (supabase.com)

  • PayMongo — Philippine payment processing (paymongo.com)

7. Cookies

NexusOS uses minimal cookies and browser local storage to maintain your session and remember your preferences (such as whether you have completed onboarding). We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We do not use third-party analytics scripts that track your behaviour across other websites.

Data we collect

Google Calendar data

When you connect Google Calendar, NexusOS requests read-only access to your primary calendar events. Calendar data is fetched in real-time in your browser and is never stored on our servers. You can disconnect at any time from Meeting Prep → Disconnect Google Calendar.
